The Robot

Overall Design

The new robot has been completely redone to accomodate needs and design flaws that were discovered in competition at the Bowling Green Falcon BEST Competition. The new design features a double-reverse-four-bar mechanism, a smaller dump container, an induction system, a specialized claw design, and a revised drive system.

Drive System


The new drive system retains the tractor wheel design of the previous model, but instead of skid wheels, it involves two omni wheels.
