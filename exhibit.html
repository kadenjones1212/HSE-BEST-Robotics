The Exhibit

Inspiration

The HSE Best Robotics exhibit is inspired by the International Space Station and other spacecrafts.

Exterior Design

The exhibit is mainly colored white with other designs like student-made solar panels, and student painted items. The curved roof of the exhibit is to make the exterior feel less boxy, just like how the ISS is round. On the roof of the exhibit, there is a student-made satellite dish to represent the many satellites on the ISS. Also waiting for you is Roary, the mascot of HSE.
