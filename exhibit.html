Community Outreach

Fall-A-Palooza


Our team hosted a stand at our local Fall-A-Palooza, where we introduced younger kids to wonders of engineering. Our team shared what the curriculum at our school is like and brought some of our projects to demonstrate. We also let them drive a robot through a course.


Tutoring Session


Our team hosted a tutoring session to give advice to younger engineering students, and assist them on projects. It also served to raise interest for non-engineers and show them what the curriculum entails.
